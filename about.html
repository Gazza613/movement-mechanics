Who We Are

Science in service
of the athlete.

Movement Mechanics is a Cape Town sports science company built on a decade of combined experience in biomechanics, athlete testing and performance monitoring - bringing research-grade tools to schools, clubs and everyday athletes.

Our Mission

Long-term athlete development, done properly.

Too many athletes are pushed toward performance outcomes before their movement foundations are ready. We exist to change that - using objective testing and evidence-based programming to guide athletes through every stage of development, from their first season to their peak years.

Every programme we run is measured, not guessed. If we can't put a number on it, we don't claim it.

Our Story

Built to close a real gap.

Movement Mechanics didn't start with a product - it started with a problem we kept seeing on the field.

01

The Need

Objective athlete testing and proper strength & conditioning were locked behind professional programmes and university labs - out of reach for school teams, community clubs and everyday athletes who needed it just as much.

02

The Approach

We apply the same scientific rigour used in elite sport - force plates, timing gates and validated test batteries - instead of guesswork, so every programme is built on measured data, not opinion.

03

The Solution

A mobile testing unit that brings that lab-grade approach to schools, clubs and homes across Cape Town - making evidence-based athlete development something every athlete can actually access, not just the elite few.

How We Work

Four principles behind every session.

Evidence-Based

Every recommendation is grounded in measured data - force plates, timing gates and validated test batteries.

Athlete-Centred

Programming is built around the individual's stage of development, not a one-size-fits-all template.

Mobile & Accessible

Our testing unit travels to you, removing the biggest barrier to getting good data: logistics.

Data Privacy

Athlete data is handled under POPIA-compliant consent processes, built specifically for school and club environments.
