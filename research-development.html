Research & Development

IMPACT: built to measure.

Our in-house R&D division exists to quantify what used to be invisible on the field. IMPACT is our flagship project - smart, load-cell-instrumented equipment that turns raw effort into real-time data.

Flagship Project

The Scrum Force System.

Built in partnership with Rhino, we instrumented a Rhino Premier Sled with four high-precision load cells to measure scrum force in real time - turning a piece of rugby hardware every club already owns into a research-grade testing device.

  • 4 high-precision load cells, 10,000N capacity each
  • 1000Hz sampling rate, ±1% force accuracy
  • Bluetooth 5.0 - streams live to the IMPACT app
  • IP67 rated, 20+ hour rechargeable battery
Live On The Sideline

From scrum to screen, in real time.

Peak force, force direction and shoulder distribution are calculated the instant the scrum engages - no lab required.

Peak Force

Instant readout of maximum force generated through the scrum, with session-over-session comparison.

Force Direction

Vertical, horizontal and lateral force breakdown to assess technique and engagement quality.

Shoulder Distribution

A heat-mapped view of force balance across the front row, left to right.

Why It Matters

Objective data, where the game happens.

For Coaches

Immediate feedback on scrum technique and effort - no more guessing who's pulling their weight.

For Athletes

Objective, individualised feedback that supports load management and long-term development.

For Research

A growing dataset that feeds directly into our sports science consultation work and academic partnerships.
